about!

I am currently a first-year PhD candidate in Stanford University's Management Science & Engineering (MS&E) program (September 2015) and completed my MA in Statistics from Harvard University (May 2015). My research interests include developing statistical methods to analyze large-scale datasets to help shape organizational policies, and working at the intersection of machine learning + engineering. In MS&E, I am a member of the SOcial ALgorithms group (SOAL) [link].
